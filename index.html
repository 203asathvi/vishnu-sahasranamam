Vishnu Sahasranamam
विष्णुसहस्रनाम

The thousand names of Lord Viṣṇu from the Mahābhārata, chanted by Bhīṣma to Yudhiṣṭhira on the battlefield of Kurukṣetra. Includes the Dhyana, Poorvangam, all 1000 names, and Phalashruthi.

🔱 Sri Rudram
Sri Rudram — Namakam
नमकम्

The Namakam is the first part of Sri Rudram from the Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda, offering salutations to Lord Rudra in all His forms and aspects. "Namas" — I bow. Each anuvaka addresses Rudra's omnipresence across all creation.

🌺 Sri Rudram
Sri Rudram — Chamakam
चमकम्

The Chamakam follows the Namakam as a prayer of supplication — "Cha me" means "and grant me this." Each anuvaka asks for blessings from food and strength to wisdom, cosmic harmony, and liberation.
